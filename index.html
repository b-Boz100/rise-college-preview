*About R.I.S.E. College

Built by CoAct. Shaped by young people.

R.I.S.E. College is founded by CoAct, a community services organisation committed to improving outcomes for young Australians. R.I.S.E. stands for Resilience, Identity, Skills and Engagement, the 4 things we believe every young person needs to thrive.

We're not building a traditional school. We're building a launchpad where young people feel known, learn through real-world experiences, earn genuine qualifications and find a direction that's theirs.

*What makes R.I.S.E. different

Three things you won't find in a typical school

Learning that connects to the real world

Hands-on projects, industry partnerships and vocational qualifications alongside academic study. Because life skills and purpose matter as much as marks.

A school built around belonging

Small by design. Trauma-informed. Relationship-centred. Every student is known by name and supported to succeed on their own terms.

Co-designed with young people

R.I.S.E. wasn't dreamed up in a boardroom. It was shaped by the young people it will serve. Their ideas, their voices, their school.

*The R.I.S.E. philosophy

What R.I.S.E. means

R

Resilience

The capacity to face challenges, recover from setbacks and keep going. At R.I.S.E. College, we build resilience through real experiences, strong relationships and a culture where it's safe to try and safe to fail.

I

Identity

Knowing who you are is the foundation for everything else. We support every student to understand their strengths, their values and the direction they want their life to take.

S

Skills

Not just academic skills, but life skills, vocational skills and the practical capabilities that open doors. Students at R.I.S.E. College earn real qualifications and real-world experience alongside their education.

E

Engagement

Learning only works when young people want to be there. How we teach, how we connect and how we support are all designed to keep young people engaged, included and invested in their own future at R.I.S.E. College.
