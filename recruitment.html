*About R.I.S.E. College

About R.I.S.E. College

R.I.S.E. College is a Special Assistance School for young people aged 14 to 18 who are disengaged from, or disadvantaged within, mainstream education. We're founded by CoAct, a community services organisation with deep roots in supporting young Australians to thrive.

R.I.S.E. stands for Resilience, Identity, Skills and Engagement. These aren't just words on a wall: they're the framework for everything we do, how we teach, how we support students and how we measure success.

We're located in Woolloongabba, Brisbane, and we open our doors on 27 January 2027.
